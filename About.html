About Me

I am a Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET) with strong experience in quality engineering, test automation, and firmware validation, working on complex enterprise and platform-level systems.

Over the years, I have contributed to end-to-end quality assurance across web applications, firmware, and platform components, acting as a quality gatekeeper for critical releases. My work spans test strategy, automation framework design, defect analysis, and release readiness, ensuring stable, reliable, and scalable systems.

I have hands-on expertise in automation using Playwright, Selenium, Robot Framework, and Python, and have successfully reduced manual testing efforts by designing automation solutions integrated into CI/CD pipelines. I enjoy improving test processes, increasing coverage, and accelerating feedback loops for engineering teams.

My background includes extensive experience in debugging and triaging complex system issues, such as BSODs, firmware failures, and platform-level logs, using tools like Windbg, Intel ITP, and NPK. I collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to analyze root causes and drive issues to closure with clear, data-backed insights.

Beyond technical execution, I value ownership, mentoring, and continuous learning. I enjoy guiding junior engineers, sharing best practices in automation and debugging, and staying current with evolving QA and testing methodologies.

I am passionate about building high-quality, reliable systems and contributing to products where quality, performance, and user trust truly matter.
